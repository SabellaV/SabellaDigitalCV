Professional Life

I have a few professional experiences in many different areas. Currently, I have an on campus job at the Costume Shop. There, I alter costumes, add details to costumes, quick rig costumes and oragnize things around the shop. We make costumes for most of the Performing Arts events. I also run a small business with my friend. We're an art business called Radiant Universe. We make handmade jewlery and decor and sell them at pop ups held by Oak Originals.

I also am Vice President of DanceWorks which is a student run dance club on campus. I also have experience with teaching dance classes. I taught classes at my old studio back in Connecticut and I am currently keeping up my skills and teaching classes here in College.

I also have done a lot of shadowing hours for Physical Therapy and have learned a lot. I just started my Cinema and Television Arts major, so I don't really have work experience for that part of my professional life yet.

Future Vision

I'm not exactly sure what I want to do in the future. I know that I want to work with children in the Physical Therapy realm, most likely in an inpatient care facility. I also know I want to write stories hopefully for TV. There's also a part of me that wants to teach at a dance studio. I know that this is a lot of things to do in the future, but I want to figure out a way to do it all. Maybe someday I'll get to do choreography.
